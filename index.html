Casc-Aid Command Center

Hyperlocal, rapid response services using an integrated platform for resilience

Hyperlocal

The command center allows you to identify and pinpoint help nearby. It cuts down response time and builds networks of people who help. The platform is build on the principle of "closest people first".

Rapid Response

Building networks between service agents and hubs cut down response time in case of emergencies. The platform let you track the reponse time and improve on it.

Services

The platform allows a range of services to be published. From emergency items to consultation services. It helps identify third party providers who can respond faster.
